World Cup Sweepstakes Generator

Create a World Cup sweepstakes draw in seconds. This free tool supports 48-team World Cup sweepstakes, FIFA-seeded draw modes, live team reveals, custom payout categories, PNG result exports, and showcase mode.

How It Works

Choose a draw format, enter player names, and randomly assign World Cup teams. The generator works for office pools, friend groups, clubs, schools, and tournament parties.

Available Draw Formats

Use one team per player for a 48-person sweepstakes, two FIFA-seeded teams per player for 24 people, or three FIFA-seeded teams per player for 16 people.
